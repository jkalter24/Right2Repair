What is Right To Repair?

In the modern day devices are not built the same way and devices break over time. Right to Repair is a vital subject today, From a consumer standpoint RTR is the ability for consumers to repair their products instead of going to their device manufacturers. It also requires companies to have easily accessible and affordable parts and access to manuals for consumers and independent tech repair companies. Currently, as of 2024, 6 states support the right to repair. Those states are IN, MA, MO, NH, VT, and WA.
